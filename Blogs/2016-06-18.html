Rotation 1

Hosted By Alex Nossek

We gathered at Alex's house one Saturday evening to have our first ever wine club, roughly 6 months after deciding to start a Wine Club!! Alex had gone to town setting out the table and making sure that there would be a clean glass for each of the wines. To get started, we had decided that it would be best to assign a wine type to each member of the group so that we would be guaranteed a varied selection. Each person also had to bring food to share that would pair well with their chosen wine. We poured tasters of each of the wines into the glasses, and loaded up our plates with different pairings. Before we got started, Matt read through guidelines on the different steps in tasting and assessing a wine. We practiced on our wines, filling in tasting sheets as we went. We had great fun trying the wine with their recommended pairings, and then also pairing them with the other food items to see how they would differ.

For the first evening, we tasted four wines. We started with the Neil Joubert Chenin Blanc 2015, followed by The Strawberry Pinot Noir Rosé 2015 and The Coral Reef Merlot 2015.

Our favourite wine for the night was the Coral Reef Merlot surprisingly. Despite a heavy nose and dark colour, this wine turned out to be light and easy to drink. Our least favourite was the Strawberry Pinot Noir Rosé. We found it quite dry and a bit on the bitter side. It did win best pairing though as it went very well with grilled chicken.

We ended off with a glass each of JC Le Roux Le Domaine sparkling wine to celebrate a successful first wine club.
