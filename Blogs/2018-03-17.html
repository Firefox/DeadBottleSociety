Unique Tastes

Hosted By Jaydon Webb


For this evening's theme we decided to explore other, unique tastes that could be identified in wines.

For example, the Boland Cellar sauvignon blanc has tastes such as litchi, pea and lime mentioned in the tasting notes. The Nitida semillon from Durbanville has fresh lemongrass, warm vanilla pod and a ligh cigar box.

Although somewhat difficult for most of us to identify, we enjoyed trying to guess the tastes and the flavour it brings to the wine!
